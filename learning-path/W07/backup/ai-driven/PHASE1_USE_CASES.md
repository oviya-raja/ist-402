# Phase 1.1: Use Cases - Job Fitment Analysis Agent

## Key Differentiator: Mental Health & Wellbeing

**The Problem:** Students face constant pressure, getting lost in tangential searches, feeling overwhelmed by options, and experiencing anxiety from uncertainty.

**The Solution:** Each use case provides one clear answer instead of endless searching, focused results instead of getting lost, and actionable plans instead of overwhelming options.

---

## 5 Core Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Find Jobs That Match My Profile from Company Websites

**What It Does:**
Automatically searches company job sites and finds jobs matching your skills and interests - not just by job title, but by what you can actually do.

**Example:**
- **Input:** Priority 1: Cisco, Google | Skills: Python, Java, AWS | Experience: Entry-level
- **Output:** 
  - Cisco: Software Engineer - New Grad (85% fit) ✅
  - Google: Software Engineer I (88% fit) ✅
- **Time Saved:** 3-4 hours → 5 minutes

**Benefits:**
- Eliminates endless browsing anxiety
- Focused, relevant results
- Reduces search fatigue

---

### Use Case 2: Check If I'm Qualified for a Specific Job

**What It Does:**
Quickly analyzes a job posting against your profile and tells you exactly how well you match and what you're missing.

**Example:**
- **Input:** Job URL at Apple - Software Engineer iOS
- **Output:** 
  - Fitment: 78% ✅ Good match, worth applying
  - Matched: Python, Swift, iOS development, CS degree
  - Missing: Objective-C (preferred), 3+ years (you have 2)
  - Recommendation: Apply! Learn basic Objective-C before interview
- **Time Saved:** 30 minutes → 2 minutes

**Benefits:**
- Eliminates self-doubt and second-guessing
- Clear, honest assessment
- Reduces decision paralysis

---

### Use Case 3: Know What Skills to Learn Before Graduation

**What It Does:**
Analyzes jobs at your target companies and tells you exactly what skills to learn to increase your chances.

**Example:**
- **Input:** Priority 1: Google, Cisco | Current Skills: Python, Java, SQL | Time: 6 months
- **Output:**
  - **Critical:** Kubernetes (2 weeks) - Required in 80% of roles, +15-20% fitment
  - **Critical:** System Design (1 month) - Essential for interviews
  - **Important:** Docker (1 week) - +10% fitment
  - **Timeline:** Month 1-2: System Design + Kubernetes | Month 3: Docker | Month 4-5: Cloud cert
- **Time Saved:** Weeks of research → Clear plan in minutes

**Benefits:**
- Eliminates "what should I learn?" overwhelm
- Prioritized learning path
- Prevents tangential learning

---

### Use Case 4: Compare Multiple Jobs to Decide Which to Apply For

**What It Does:**
Compares multiple jobs side-by-side and tells you which ones are worth your time.

**Example:**
- **Input:** 4 job URLs (2 Google, 1 Cisco, 1 Apple) + Priority list
- **Output:**
  - #1: Google - Software Engineer I (92% fit) ✅ APPLY FIRST
  - #2: Cisco - Software Engineer - New Grad (85% fit) ✅ APPLY
  - #3: Google - Associate SE (78% fit) ⚠️ MAYBE
  - #4: Apple - iOS Engineer (72% fit) ❌ SKIP FOR NOW
- **Time Saved:** Hours of comparison → 5 minutes

**Benefits:**
- Eliminates decision paralysis
- Clear ranking and prioritization
- Saves mental energy

---

### Use Case 5: Get a Complete Job Search Plan

**What It Does:**
Creates a complete, personalized job search strategy with skill development, application timeline, and recommendations.

**Example:**
- **Input:** Profile + Target Companies (Priority 1: Google, Cisco) + Timeline: 4 months
- **Output:**
  - **Phase 1 (Month 1-2):** Learn Kubernetes, Master System Design
  - **Phase 2 (Month 2):** Update resume, prepare portfolio
  - **Phase 3 (Month 3):** Apply to Priority 1 companies (5 roles)
  - **Phase 4 (Month 4):** Interview prep, follow-ups
  - **Recommended Roles:** Ranked list with fitment scores
- **Time Saved:** Days of planning → 10 minutes

**Benefits:**
- Transforms overwhelming chaos into manageable plan
- Reduces job search anxiety
- Clear roadmap eliminates uncertainty
