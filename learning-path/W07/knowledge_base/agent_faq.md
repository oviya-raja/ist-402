## Week Concepts

**Q: What should I learn in Week 0?**

---

**Q: What should I learn in Week 1?**

**A:** Week 1 (Prompt Engineering) covers: LLM Fundamentals (how LLMs work), Prompt Design Patterns (zero-shot, few-shot, chain-of-thought), Temperature and Sampling. These concepts teach you how to effectively interact with and prompt LLMs. Ask me to explain concepts or test your understanding!

---

**Q: What should I learn in Week 2?**

**A:** Week 2 (RAG Foundations) covers: Data Engineering for RAG, Embeddings Deep Dive, Embedding Models Comparison, Chunking Strategies, and Vector Database Fundamentals. These concepts are essential for building retrieval-augmented generation systems. Ask me to explain or test you!

---

**Q: What should I learn in Week 3?**

**A:** Week 3 (RAG Production) covers: Retrieval Strategies (hybrid search, reranking), Context Window Management, RAG Evaluation Metrics, and Hallucination Detection. These are production-ready RAG concepts. Ask me to explain concepts or create a study plan!

---

**Q: What should I learn in Week 4?**

**Q: What should I learn in Week 5?**

**Q: What should I learn in Week 6?**

**Q: What should I learn in Week 7?**

**Q: What should I learn in Week 8?**

**Q: What should I learn in Week 9?**
