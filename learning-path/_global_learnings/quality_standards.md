## Quality Standards

*These standards are refined based on experience across multiple assignments.*

---

**Last Updated:** 2025-11-29T17:05:00Z

### Universal Quality Principles:

1. **Completeness:** All requirements must be met
2. **Professionalism:** Work must be professionally presented
3. **Clarity:** Communication must be clear and well-organized
4. **Accuracy:** No errors or typos
5. **Alignment:** Must align with rubric/requirements

### Quality Score Guidelines:

- **5/5:** Exceeds expectations, exceptional quality
- **4/5:** Meets all requirements, high quality
- **3/5:** Meets requirements, acceptable quality
- **2/5:** Missing requirements or quality issues
- **1/5:** Significant gaps or problems

## Quality Standards Evolution

### Update: 2025-11-29 - Pattern Analysis Integration

**Source:** Self-assessment weakness pattern analysis from W7 assignment

**New Standards Added:**

1. **Quantitative Data Requirement:**
   - Always include statistics, metrics, or percentages when possible
   - Support claims with quantitative evidence
   - If data unavailable, note it explicitly

2. **Visual Aids Consideration:**
   - Consider diagrams, maps, or charts for complex concepts
   - Use visual aids when text alone is insufficient
   - Include flowcharts for processes, user journey maps for users

3. **Specificity and Context:**
   - Provide specific context, not just general descriptions
   - Address edge cases and special considerations
   - Include accessibility and inclusivity requirements

4. **Comparative Analysis:**
   - Reference similar solutions or approaches when relevant
   - Include industry benchmarks
   - Validate against existing solutions

**Updated Quality Score Guidelines:**
- **5/5:** Includes quantitative data, visual aids (if needed), specific context, comparative analysis
- **4/5:** Meets all requirements, high quality, may be missing some enhancements above
- **3/5:** Meets requirements, acceptable quality
- **2/5:** Missing requirements or quality issues
- **1/5:** Significant gaps or problems
